REEACT has been founded with people emotions and feelings as a priority. Feeling happy about your choices in life is something that can be considered somewhat challenging, and in the long term can create a dissatisfaction in how we perceive our impacts in society. REAACT wants to help people to discover choicesthey have in everyday life, and little by little imrprove tremendously their lives. The website comes from the mind of Sara Leoni, a student from CF:G Code first:girls during her first project. Sara is a Business Psychology undergraduate student who is a strong believer in learning as a way to become more receptive to the choices we have.
